Questions

Fair things to ask first.

Will this make me money? +
No one can honestly promise that, and anyone who does is selling you something. Trading involves real risk and you can lose money. What this gives you is a proper foundation and a clear process — which is what separates people who last from people who don't.
I'm a complete beginner. Is that okay? +
It's ideal, actually. The whole course is built to start from zero and add one layer at a time. Beginners who haven't picked up bad habits often move faster.
Why not give me the strategy now? +
Because a strategy without the basics underneath it is just another signal you'd be following blindly. The first four lessons are what make the fifth one usable. Do them and Lesson 5 unlocks.
Do you give signals or alerts? +
No, and that's on purpose. The goal is for you to read the chart yourself. Signals make you dependent; skills make you independent.
How much time does it take? +
As much or as little as you want — it's self-paced. The lessons are short and meant to be practised on live charts between each one, not binged in a weekend.
Can I cancel? +
